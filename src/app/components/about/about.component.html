About Us (testing text)

The University of Leicester Green Party Society is a student-led group for those interested in environmental justice, social equality, and progressive political change.

We provide a space for students to discuss Green values, learn about political and environmental issues, and take part in campaigns and activities both on campus and beyond and around Leicester.

Our place in the wider Green movement

We are affiliated with the Young Greens, the youth and student branch of the Green Party of England and Wales, which exists to empower young people to engage with politics and shape a more sustainable and just future. As a university society, our focus is on creating an accessible space for students to learn about political issues, share ideas, develop confidence, and take part in meaningful discussion and action, regardless of prior political experience.

Our society works independently while maintaining links with local and national Green Party groups, guest speakers, and campaigns.

What we do

  • Host talks, discussions, and workshops
  • Organise social and campaigning events
  • Promote environmental and political awareness on campus
  • Create a welcoming space for debate and learning

Who can join

Our society is open to all University of Leicester students and you do not need to be a Green Party member to take part.

Whether you are deeply involved in politics or simply curious about sustainability and social issues, you are welcome to join us.

Get involved

You can get involved by attending events, joining discussions, or helping organise activities throughout the year.

Keep an eye on our events page and blog for updates, or follow us on social media to stay informed.
