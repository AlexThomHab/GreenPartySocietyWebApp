Why Students Should Care About Local Climate Policy

By Alex · 3 February 2025

Climate change is often framed as a global issue, but many of the most impactful decisions are made locally — by councils, universities, and community organisations.

For students, local climate policy affects housing standards, transport options, energy use on campus, and access to green spaces.

Engaging locally is often more accessible than national politics, and collective student voices can have real influence.

Getting involved doesn’t require expertise — just curiosity, openness, and willingness to act together.
